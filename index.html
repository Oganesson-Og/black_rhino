The Heritage Collection Black Rhino Figurine

From the Heart of Zimbabwe

Artisan's Masterpiece

Brought to the Group IT department by Keith Satuku, this exceptional piece represents the pinnacle of Zimbabwean craftsmanship. Hand-carved from rare verdite stone, it embodies strength and resilience.

The Stone of Africa

Verdite stone, a semi-precious mineral predominantly found in Zimbabwe, has been treasured for centuries. This rare stone carries the legacy of African artistry and cultural significance.

Its vibrant green hues symbolize growth and vitality, making it a perfect medium for capturing the essence of African wildlife.

Symbol of Strength

Natural Heritage

The black rhino, critically endangered yet resilient, represents the enduring spirit of African wildlife. Its presence in Zimbabwe's landscapes tells a story of survival and adaptation.

Conservation Journey
